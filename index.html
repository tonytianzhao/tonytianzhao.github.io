Tian at a glance

I was born and grew up in Yangzhou, a 2,501-year-old Chinese city of great reputation in the Grand Canal and delicate Huaiyang cuisine. In 2013 I was admitted by Nanjing University, working towards a B.S. in Geographical Sciences. Meanwhile, I have been serving as the Class Monitor to speak for my class since September 2014.

It is Professor Shu GAO who led me into the fascinating research of coastal oceanography. Currently I am working with Prof. Shu GAO, Prof. Yaping WANG and Dr. Qian YU on sediment dynamics.
